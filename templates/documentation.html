Models Explanation

This application uses six different random forest regression models to find the average of their wRC+ predictions. The parameters used for the models were RandomForestRegressor(max_depth=10, max_features='sqrt', min_samples_split=5, n_estimators=150, n_jobs=-1, random_state=42) with each model trained on a different grouping of Baseball Savant metrics.
